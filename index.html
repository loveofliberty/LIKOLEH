About LIKOLEH

Founded in 2014 in Man, Tonkpi Region, LIKOLEH CI is a registered Ivorian NGO promoting economic empowerment, child protection, education and peacebuilding across Côte d’Ivoire.

Our Work

Education

Supporting access to education for children in rural communities through school programs and resources.

Women Empowerment

Promoting economic independence and leadership opportunities for women across local communities.

Child Protection

Protecting vulnerable children and supporting safe environments for growth and education.
